THREADS OF CONNECTION

These prints blend the bold lines of the Bandra-Worli Sea Link with the delicate heritage of Warli art, reflecting Mumbai's quiet balance of tradition and modernity. To me, the Sea Link speaks of bridges—both real and personal—built while finding my place in a new city. Printed on pillows, they carry a sense of comfort and belonging, a reminder to embrace the contrasts and connections that make Mumbai feel like home.

AANKHON KA TAARA

Designed a series of eye-catching advertisements for Poshan Skincare, focusing on their eye serum. The visuals highlighted the product's premium quality and gentle care, combining clean layouts with soothing colour palettes to convey trust and effectiveness.

SANDS OF FAITH

A hand-painted board game set in a magical desert, where shifting sands mirror life's balance between challenge and opportunity. Featuring three unique sets of playing cards and a water token system, the game's objective is to reach the end point with 20 water points. Rooted in the spirit of Impressionism, it invites players to lose themselves in fleeting beauty, layered storytelling, and the joy of creative exploration.

DRAMA LLAMA PACKAGING

A playful coffee packaging design that turns a simple unboxing into a moment of discovery. With its twistable, interactive form, it engages the senses, sparking curiosity and delight while capturing the brand's quirky, spirited personality.
